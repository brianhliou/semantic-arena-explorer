Every board word and every legal clue is a 300-dimensional GloVe word vector. Pick the cards you want your partner to find; the browser ranks all 25 board words by cosine similarity to each of the ~7,000 legal clue words and reports the single clue that best separates your set.

Green means some legal word ranks all of your targets above every other card. Red means none does — the wall. The number is the projection margin: how far the best clue is from cleanly separating the set.

The second tab, Decoder-perfect, reader fails, replays real audits: clues the decoder rated perfect that a live language-model or human reader still couldn't recover. Decoder-recoverable and humanly readable come apart.
